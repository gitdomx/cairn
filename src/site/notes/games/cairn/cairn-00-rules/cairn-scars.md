# Scars
- if damage to PC reduces HP to exactly zero
	- look up result based on amount taken
## Scars table
| HP Lost | Result                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1       | **Lasting Scar.** Roll `dice:1d6`. 1: Neck, 2: Hands, 3: Eye, 4: Chest, 5: Legs, 6: Ear. Roll `dice:1d6`. If the total is higher than your max HP, take the new result.                                                              |
| 2       | **Rattling Blow.** You’re disoriented and shaken. Describe how you refocus. Roll `dice:1d6`. If the total is higher than your max HP, take the new result.                                                                           |
| 3       | **Walloped.** You’re sent flying and land flat on your face, winded. You are deprived until you rest for a few hours. Then, roll `dice:1d6`. Add that amount to your max HP.                                                         |
| 4       | **Broken Limb.** Roll `dice:1d6`. 1-2: Leg, 3-4: Arm, 5: Rib, 6: Skull. Once mended, roll `dice:2d6`. If the total is higher than your max HP, take the new result.                                                                  |
| 5       | **Diseased.** You’re afflicted with a gross, uncomfortable infection. When you get over it, roll `dice:2d6`. If the total is higher than your max HP, take the new result.                                                           |
| 6       | **Reorienting Head Wound.** Roll `dice:1d6`. 1-2: STR, 3-4: DEX, 5-6: WIL. Roll `dice:3d6`. If the total is higher than your current attribute, take the new result.                                                                 |
| 7       | **Hamstrung.** You can barely move until you get serious help and rest. After recovery, roll `dice:3d6`. If the total is higher than your max DEX, take the new result.                                                              |
| 8       | **Deafened.** You cannot hear anything until you find extraordinary aid. Regardless, make a WIL save `dice:1d20`. If you pass, increase your max WIL by `dice:1d4`.                                                                  |
| 9       | **Re-brained.** Some hidden part of your psyche is knocked loose. Roll `dice:3d6`. If the total is higher than your max WIL, take the new result.                                                                                    |
| 10      | **Sundered.** An appendage is torn off, crippled, or useless. (The Warden will tell you which.) Then make a WIL save `dice:1d20`. If you pass, increase your max WIL by `dice:1d6`.                                                  |
| 11      | **Mortal Wound.** You are deprived and out of action. You die in one hour unless healed. Upon recovery, roll `dice:2d6`. Take the new result as your max HP.                                                                         |
| 12      | **Doomed.** Death seemed ever so close, but somehow you survived. If your next save against critical damage is a fail, you die horribly. If you pass, roll `dice:3d6`. If the total is higher than your max HP, take the new result. |
