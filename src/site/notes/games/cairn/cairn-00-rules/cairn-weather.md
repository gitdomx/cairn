# Weather in Cairn
- [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-warden\|Warden]] to roll everyday
- if "Extreme" rolled twice in a row
	- weather turns "Catastrophic"
- keep track of weather rolls

## Weather Tables

### Weather Type
Roll: `dice:[[cairn-weather^cairn-weather]]`

| **1d6** | **Spring** | **Summer** | **Fall**   | **Winter** |
| ------ | ---------- | ---------- | ---------- | ---------- |
| 1      | Nice       | Nice       | Fair       | Fair       |
| 2      | Fair       | Nice       | Fair       | Unpleasant |
| 3      | Fair       | Fair       | Unpleasant | Inclement  |
| 4      | Unpleasant | Unpleasant | Inclement  | Inclement  |
| 5      | Inclement  | Inclement  | Inclement  | Extreme    |
| 6      | Extreme    | Extreme    | Extreme    | Extreme    |{ #cairn-weather}


### Weather Difficulty
|**Weather**|**Effect**|**Examples**|
|---|---|---|
|Nice|Favorable conditions for travel.|_Clear skies, sunny_|
|Fair|Favorable conditions for travel.|_Overcast, breezy_|
|Unpleasant|Add a [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Fatigue\|fatigue]] or add one [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Watches\|watch]] to the journey.|_Gusting winds, rain showers, sweltering heat, chill air_|
|Inclement|Add a [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Fatigue\|fatigue]] or add one [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Watches\|watch]]. Increase [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-path-difficulty#Terrain Difficulty\|terrain difficulty]] by a step.|_Thunderstorms, lightning, rain, muddy ground_|
|Extreme|Add a [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Fatigue\|fatigue]] and add one [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Watches\|watch]]. Increase [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-path-difficulty#Terrain Difficulty\|terrain difficulty]] by a step.|_Blizzards, freezing winds, flooding, mud slides_|
|Catastrophic|Most parties cannot travel under these conditions.|_Tornados, tidal waves, hurricane, volcanic eruption_|
