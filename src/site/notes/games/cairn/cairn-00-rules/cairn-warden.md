# Warden in Cairn

## World Building

### Weather
- refer to [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-weather#Weather Tables\|weather tables]]
- roll start of day
- if "Extreme" rolled twice in a row
	- weather turns "Catastrophic"
- keep track of weather rolls
## NPCs

### Combat
#### Morale (NPC)
- WIL save
	- when taking their first casualty
	- when losing half their side
	- flee on failure

#### Detachments (NPC)
- large groups of similar enemies fighting as one
	- treated as a single **detachment**
- when detachment takes critical damage
	- the damage is routed or significantly weakened
- attacks from detachments are
	- [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Enhanced\|enhanced]]
	- have [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Blast\|blast]]
- attacks against detachments are
	- [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Impaired\|impaired]] (excluding blast damage)
