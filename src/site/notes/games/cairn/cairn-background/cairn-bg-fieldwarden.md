> [!summary] Protectors of the harvest, defense against pests, thieves, and beasts. A position of great honor, while it lasts: many guardians do not live out their natural lives. Roll a second time on the **Bonds** table.

## Starting Gear
- 3d6 Gold Pieces
- Rations (3 uses)
- Torch (3 uses)
- Brigandine (1 Armor, _bulky_)
- Sling (d6)
- Hand Axe (d6)
- Repellent (pick the type, 3 uses)

## What got the better of you?
| 1d6   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | 
| ----- | ------|
| **1** | A voracious swarm of pests that swallowed crops and animals alike. With nothing to defend, you left. Take **Gale Seed Extract** (3 uses). Ingesting it lets you sprint with a speed four times your regular rate. Afterward you add two [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Fatigue\|Fatigue]].                          |
| **2** | A crop spirit, angered by a poor tithing. The fires consumed nearly everything, and afterward you were able to gather a pouch of **Fireseeds** (d8, _blast_, 4 uses).                                                                                                    |
| **3** | An antlered, toothy demon that nearly ended you. Take a blood-stained **Bone Knife** (d6). On **Critical Damage**, its next attack becomes _enhanced_ from contact with blood.                                                          |
| **4** | _The Withering_, a type of stem rot from the **Roots**. Take a **Diseased Crop** (6 uses) that quickly decays any plant it touches.                                                                                                                    |
| **5** | Wolves, or werewolves. You are now a **Werewolf** 8 HP, 15 STR, 14 DEX, claws (d6+d6), bite (d8). WIL remains the same. You can _turn_ at will (once per day) but must make a WIL save to revert. Anyone left alive from your attacks - make a WIL save to avoid infection.     |
| **6** | Crop thieves. Not all of them survived, but you were outnumbered. Start with +d4 HP and a **Cusped Falchion** (d8).                              |{ #cairn-fieldwarden-1}


## What saved your life?
| 1d6 | Item | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | **Bloodvine Whip** | d8 damage. On [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Critical Damage\|critical damage]], it drains the target’s blood, granting the weapon’s next attack the _blast_ quality |
| **2** | **Clatter Keeper** | A hand-cranked device that emits a loud noise, frightening away most creatures. |
| **3** | **Sun Stick** | Provides ample warmth and light for up to one hour. [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Relics\|Recharge]]: Leave in heavy sunlight for a full day. 1 use. |
| **4** | **Root Tether** | When thrown, binds a creature as large as a wolf to the soil for a short time. |
| **5** | **Greenwhistle** | A small flute that calms plants, making passage through areas heavy with plant life a bit easier. |
| **6** | **Everbloom Band** | A circlet adorned with flowers that never wilt. On [[games/cairn/cairn-00-rules/cairn-basics#Critical Damage\|critical damage]], the flowers dissolve into dust, but you act as if your save succeeded (STR loss still occurs). |{ #cairn-fieldwarden-item}
